Forum Vijaya Mall is a shopping mall located in Vadapalani, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, developed by Prestige Group.[1][2] This mall is spread over 6,36,000 square feet of retail space. More than 100 shops occupy its four floors. This mall distinguishes itself by being local by housing Chennai-based brands in the mall such as Spar Hypermarket, RmKV, Lifestyle, Max, Marks & Spencer’s, Westside, Fab India and Via South. It is also home to SPI Cinema’s Palazzo multiplex and an IMAX screen as well.[3]

Shops[edit]

The Forum Vijaya from the inside

In terms of shops, each floor has been categorized such as ground floor for formal wear, first floor for casual & ethnic wear, second floor for Electronic and Travel and third floor for cinemas and food court . Some of the branded shops in the mall are Lifestyle,Park Avenue[4], Louis Philipe,Arrow, Ruosh, Basics, Levi's, Hidesign, Reebok, Nike, Addidas, Puma, Wildcraft, Soch,Indian Terrain,W,Jockey,Melange,Biba,Kalanikethan,Funskool,Van Heusen,Manyavar, US Polo Assn., UCB, Colorplus, Firstcry.com, Mothercare,HP, Lenovo, VIP, Witco, Bata, Gini&Jony.

Entertainment[edit]

  • This mall houses an 8-screen multiplex from SPI Cinemas titled Palazzo and also one IMAX screen, the second in the city.[5]
  • Fun city a gaming and entertainment zone for kids located at level 3 and 4.[6]
  • Forum Express, Kiddie Rides, VR Games etc.

Food and dining[edit]

Forum Mall's food court named as Via South indicates that most of the food stalls here have southern blend into their food. This food court has an 650-seat capacity, and major F&B's like KFC, McDonald's, Junior Kuppanna, Galito’s, Taco Bell, Burger King, Beijing Bites, Tuk Tuk, Dosa & co, Wow! Momo, Pandikadai, Arabian Hut, Amaravathi, Tibbs Frankie, Ibaco, Squeez Juice Bars, Ibakes,Sandwich Square, Madras Coffee House, Domino's Pizza, Cookieman, Krispy Kreme, Cafe Coffee Day, Kalmane Koffees, Salt restaurant, Cream & Fudge, Subway, Bombaysthan, The Belgian Waffle Co, The pasta bar Venetto, ID, Cream centre are located in Forum Vijaya mall.

Past events

  • Vipul Goyal, the star of TVF’s hugely popular web series Humorously Yours, performed at Forum Vijaya Mall, Vadapalani! Having delivered many laugh riots through his shows globally and with more than 10 million stand-up video views online, some of his jokes and punchlines have even gained cult status. 200+ guests were thoroughly entertained by his performance!
  • MOP Vaishnav College unveils Pagir at Forum Chennai.This is an initiative where nearly 250 students gathered from their Business Administration department to educate guests on alternatives to plastic usage for a greener city.
  • 150+ people participated in the Cookie eating contest at Forum Vijaya Mall that took place today! Top 3 winners were given a year long Subscription from Cookieman.
  • Forum Rock on *Grand Finale* witnessed some of the best performances by finalists in the Rock, Pop and Fusion categories! The competition was judged by playback singers Rahul Nambiar and Christopher Stanley and prizes worth Rs 3 lakhs were given out to winners!
  • Guinness World Record for the tallest tower of cupcakes at Forum Vijaya Mall.The 41.8-feet tower at Forum Vijaya Mall set the Guinness World Record for the tallest cupcake tower, beating the previous record, which stood at 35 feet which was held by a South African organisation.
  • Forum Shopathon 2019 - Forum Vijaya Mall hosted Shopathon, a 45-day long rewarding program for shoppers. While Mr Chinnadurai and family walked home with a Maruti Ignis, 450 other families won gift vouchers amounting to 3.5+ lakhs. Shopping at Forum Chennai is always a rewarding experience!
  • Forum Colorama - an annual property by Forum Malls across the country This was a drawing competition was themed “Agriculture in India” for children aged between 4 to 16 years. The participants were categorized into ages groups of 4-6 years, 7-9 years, 10-13 years and 14-16 years and the best 3 drawings from each age group were given exciting prizes.
